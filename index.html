For Safety Departments

Injury Case Management

When an injury happens, time matters. PGS provides a full-service, 24/7 injury management solution that keeps your people safe and your OSHA logs clean.

✓

24/7 Injury Triage

Round-the-clock nurse triage line to assess injuries in real time and direct appropriate care — day or night.
